Personal Prayer for Inner Peace

Lord, grant me the strength to forgive myself

For the world is cruel and the doubt of trust is high.

Provide me the patience to endure the hardships of mankind

As I walk by on the journey called life and the problems it shall throw me,

I wish for your patience towards me, Good Lord.

For even your own chosen people use your name to justify their misdeeds towards others.

Protection and guidance is all I seek and ask from You.

And if provided as such, I shall be eternally grateful.

Provide not only for me, but to those who are in need as well in these trying times that we are in.

All these we ask in your most Holy Name,


Amen
